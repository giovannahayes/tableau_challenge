Jersey City CitiBike Ridership Dashboard

Findings

Grove Street PATH Station is the Station where the most journeys have originated
The majority of the top ten stations of where journeys originated is near the waterfront
Top ten stations where journeys ended are not different from where they originated
Columbus Drive Station is the only station that was delisted from a top ten journey origination ranking over the course of 2019 & 2020

Male riders outnumber female riders 2 to 1

Liberty Light Rail riders have the longest trip duration on average. Their average trip lasts 46 minutes. 
Jersey and 6th Station riders have the shortest trip duration on average. Their average trip lasts less than 10 minutes
Average trip of a CitiBike rider is approximately 14 minutes.
